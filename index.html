An AI agent that lives on your laptop.

Open-source, native, and private. Athen reads your inbox, watches your calendar, and quietly handles the routine — only interrupting when something genuinely needs your call.

It knows when to ask.

Replying to a known contact happens silently. Sending email to a stranger? It surfaces the draft and asks first — over the app or on Telegram if you're away.

It remembers you.

Past conversations, who's who in your contacts, your preferences. You stop re-explaining yourself every session.

It works with your own AI.

Plug in a Claude or ChatGPT key — or run a free local model on your own machine. Nothing leaves your computer unless you want it to.
